The "col-md" class is marked for medium-sized screens and desktop, while "col-sm-2" is marked for small screens. So, when you reduce the browser size, the "col-md" row will break first because it is optimized for larger screens. The "col-sm-2" row will adapt to smaller screens, making the city names more likely to fit without breaking.
